About Our Sanctuary

A Luxury Haven Crafted for True Serenity

Welcome to The Parasol Inn Sikkim, a luxury destination nestled amidst the towering clouds and majestic cliffs of Gangtok. Built with standard mountain architecture and natural stones, the resort offers modern aesthetics blended seamlessly with regional Tibetan-Buddhist style accents.

Relax, recharge, and breathe in the crisp Himalayan breeze. Here, every balcony overlooks the dramatic snow-peaks, and our boutique services are custom-tailored to provide you with an unforgettable mountain getaway.
